# Explainers

Plain-language explanations of technical frameworks for general audiences.

### Documents in this folder:

**Understanding_SIP-_A_Simple_Breakdown.docx**  
Accessible explanation of Sentient Infrastructure Protocol designed for family/friends - covers the three layers (Material, Political, Mythic/Spiritual) without technical jargon

**Understanding_the_Future_We_re_Building_Together.docx**  
Comprehensive vision document connecting XRP/XLM, SIP, and The Baltic Corridor - explains how AI and humanity can grow together ethically

### Purpose:

These documents translate complex technical and governance frameworks into language accessible to non-technical audiences. They maintain the depth and importance of the work while removing barriers to understanding.

### Audience:

- Family members who want to understand what you're working on
- Friends outside technical fields
- Potential collaborators who need high-level context before diving into technical docs
- Anyone curious about AI governance and transparent economic systems

### Approach:

Rather than dumbing down the concepts, these explainers use analogies, clear structure, and narrative framing to make sophisticated ideas comprehensible without sacrificing accuracy.
